Blackpink

Blackpink in your area!


Blackpink is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé and Lisa.The group debuted on August 8, 2016, with their single album Square One, which spawned "Whistle", their first number-one song in South Korea, as well as "Boombayah", their first number-one hit on the Billboard World Digital Songs chart, which set the record as the most-viewed debut music video by a Korean act.



BLACKPINK is a brand new girl group, fresh to the K-pop scene. The group was created by YG Entertainment, the same company as the man who brought you Gangnam Style by Hallyu star Psy. BLACKPINK is only the second girl group that the company has trained and put together, but they are already taking Korea (and many other countries, for that matter) by a storm. The group is comprised of four members: Lisa, Jennie, Jisoo and Rose. The group has released four singles so far, with the most viewed of which being "Boombayah," towering with over 89 million views to date —a huge number compared to other K-pop artists let alone for a rookie group.
Here's the 101 on who's who in this squad:

Lisa Manoban



Position: Lead rapper, main dancer, sub-vocalist and maknae (youngest)!

Lisa is the youngest member of the group. She was born in Thailand on March 27, 1997. She has been training with YG Entertainment for about five years and finally debuted in 2016. She is fluent in multiple languages, including Thai, Korean and English. Leading up to her debut last year, she featured with other YG artists like B.I and Bobby of iKon. She was even a member of a small group called We Zaa Cool with Bambam of GOT7.


Jennie Kim




Position: Main rapper and sub-vocalist.
Jennie was born in South Korea, but is known for having studied abroad in New Zealand before auditioning for a spot in YG Entertainment. Like the other members of the group, she is also fluent in English alongside the native Korean language. She was born January 16, 1996. She became rapper and vocalist within the group after training for more than five years.

Rosé Park




Position: Main vocalist.
Rosé was born in New Zealand but raised in Melbourne, Australia. She can also speak both English and Korean. She was born February 11, 1997 and is in charge of vocals. She has worked as a YG trainee for over four years leading to her debut. She has appeared in G-Dragon's music video, "Without You," as he is under the same company as BLACKPINK.


Jisoo Kim



Position: Visual (face of the group) and lead vocalist.
Born and raised in South Korea, Jisoo is the fourth member of the group. She was born January 11, 1995, making her the eldest of the four girls. She slays vocals, and she, as with all the other members, is also responsible for dancing intricate choreography. Like the rest of the members, she is able to speak multiple languages.
